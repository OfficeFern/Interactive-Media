Pick'em Ups (Name Pending)


Hello! Welcome to my landing page for my app idea, "Pick'em Ups". This landing page is a work and progress that will be refined as I learn Bootstrap to make the CSS look a lot cleaner.

The Problem

One of the issues teens face when they move away from their childhood homes is making new friends. It can be intimidating to try and figure out where to or how to meet new people without being uncomfortable. So I was looking for a way to make it easier for these teens to connect with others that have similar interests or that just want to meet new people.

The Solution

So my idea took form of the app concept, "Pick'em Ups". Pick'em Ups is a social app that, in short, allows its users to create or find local pick-up games with other users, but I'll go into more detail as we go on.

To start off I wanted to create a easy to navigate home page so it wasn't confusing to younger users. The content of the tabs will change as more research is done. In these sketches though I include how the map page may look and how a user would see the markers/game locations. There is also and idea of how it'd look when you click on one of the game markers. The friends tab is the first draft of how you'd see your friends organized and how a persons profile would look when you select their name.
